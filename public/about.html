About the Club

Our chapter at The Ohio State University, established in 2023, is driven by highly accomplished students who are passionate about representation in tech. We currently have over 200 registered and active members majoring in Computer Science, Engineering, Business, and Data Analytics. We are the largest ColorStack chapter and our community continues to grow.

Founded in 2021, ColorStack at Ohio State proudly stands as the second chapter of the national ColorStack organization. Our mission is rooted deeply in the empowerment and support of Black, Latinx, and Indigenous students navigating the world of computing. ColorStack at Ohio State serves as a home and resource for those who are in tech majors such as Computer Science, Data Science, Information Science, and Computer Engineering.

Through our initiatives, we aim to increase the success of our members by facilitating access to internship opportunities, fellowships, and conferences such as Tapia, Grace Hopper, and AfroTech.
